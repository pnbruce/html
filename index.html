Tic-Tac-Toe!

How to play

  1. The first player to move chooses a square to place an X.
  2. The second player to move chooses different square to place an O.
  3. The first and second player will alternate until one of them has won or there are no remaining squares.
  4. The first player wins if the board has 3 X's in a row.
  5. The second player wins if the board has 3 O's in a row.
